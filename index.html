I am a senior undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur interested in various aspects of Computer Science on the Theoretical side. I am also interested in application of algorithms for Social good. I was previously at Microsoft Research and was part of a project aimed at using Machine Learning for first response during disaster situations. I have worked to make AI algorithms for speech more accessible by developing models for accented speech generation. More of my recent work has been on the theoretical side where I have been exploring Algorithmic notions of Randomness and Ergodic systems. I have also been looking at optimization techniques that use Langevin dynamics and other processes for convex and non convex functions. I also have been looking at graph sparsification using Spectral Graph theory. For information checkout any of the recent blog posts or the projects page

Nothing is more practical than a good theory!
